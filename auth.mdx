## Authetication:

### Create an Account On Hamsa Dashboard:

<br />

<Card
  title="Hamsa Dashboard"
  icon="browser"
  href="https://dashboard-dev.tryhamsa.com/signup"
  >
    <br />
    Click here to create an account on Hamsa Dashboard
  </Card>

<br />

<li>First, you need to create an account from the link in the above card.</li>
<li>After creating an account, you will recieve an email directs you to the login page.</li>
<li>Now, you can sign in to your account using the same email and password you used.</li>
<li>You can use <b>Continue with Google</b> option to signup with using Google Auth.</li>
